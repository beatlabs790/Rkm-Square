Frequently Asked Questions

What is the completion status of the property?

Shivam RKM Square is an entirely completed ready-to-move apartment complex. Occupancy and residential possession records successfully began in July 2021.

Is there commercial retail on-site?

Yes, RKM Square is a prominent mixed-use development. The lower commercial tiers house an operational Domino's Pizza store open daily for dining and takeaway.

What size formats are the apartments built in?

The property features classic 3 BHK and 4 BHK residential apartments. Net covered carpet dimensions range between 882 sq.ft. to 900 sq.ft.
